/* Header height — reserved for Phase 16 sticky header. ... */

Replace hardcoded `rounded-lg` and `rounded-md` on primary **card containers** (the outer `div` wrappers that form card/panel surfaces with `bg-surface-elevated`, `shadow-md`, or `border`) with `rounded-(card)` (the Tailwind CSS-variable utility for `--card-radius`).

**Scope rule:** Only replace `rounded-*` on card-level container elements (the outermost `div` of a card). Do NOT change:
- `rounded-full` (circles/avatars — unchanged)
- `rounded-lg` on buttons or form inputs (they use a separate button/input radius)
- Small inline alert `rounded-md` divs inside cards (leave those as-is for visual hierarchy)

**Files and specific replacements:**

`frontend/app/scan/[id]/page.tsx` — 4 outer card divs:

`frontend/app/results/[token]/page.tsx` — 3 outer card divs:

`frontend/app/results/[token]/loading.tsx` — outer card divs:
